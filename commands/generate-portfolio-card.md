name: generate-portfolio-card
description: Generate an embeddable portfolio card HTML that links to your case study, marketplace, or signup form.
---

Run the `generate-portfolio-card` skill workflow.

Generates an HTML card fragment you can paste into your portfolio. The card inherits your site's font and colors, and the button links wherever you specify (full case study, Cursor Marketplace, email form, etc.).

Output: `.case-study/output/portfolio-card.html`
